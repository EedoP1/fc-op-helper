<objective>
Refactor the backtesting engine to use a single-pass timeline walk across all strategy+param combos instead of rebuilding and re-walking the timeline 129 times independently.

Purpose: The current approach takes ~2 hours because timeline construction (defaultdict + sort of 727k price points) runs once per combo. Single-pass does it once, reducing total runtime roughly proportional to combo count.

Output: New `run_sweep_single_pass()` function in engine.py, updated `run_cli()`, tests proving equivalence.
</objective>
